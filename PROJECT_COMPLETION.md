## 📖 Course Contents

### Unit I: Introduction to Tourism & Hospitality Management
- Meaning and concept of management
- Nature of management (Science, Art, Profession)
- Functions of management (POSDC model)
- Relevance in tourism industry

### Unit II: Transportation Management
- Types of transportation (Air, Rail, Road, Water)
- Transportation planning and coordination
- Customer service in transportation
- Service quality standards

### Unit III: Accommodation Management
- Hotel classification and ratings (1-5 star systems)
- Front office operations
- Housekeeping department management
- Food & Beverage operations
- Guest service excellence

### Unit IV: Human Resource Management
- HRM fundamentals and importance
- Recruitment and selection process
- Training and development programs
- Performance management
- Compensation and employee relations
- Case study: Hilton Worldwide HRM practices

### Unit V: Destination Management
- Destination concepts and components
- Destination planning and development
- Sustainable tourism principles
- Stakeholder management
- Case study: Kyoto, Japan

### Unit VI: Current Trends in Tourism & Hospitality
- Artificial Intelligence and chatbots
- Virtual and Augmented Reality
- Contactless technologies
- Green hospitality and eco-tourism
- Experience economy and personalization
- Post-pandemic challenges and recovery
