📚 About This Course

Management for Tourism and Hospitality (HMT712) is a foundational course designed to equip students with essential knowledge and skills in managing tourism and hospitality operations. This course provides comprehensive insights into various aspects of the tourism industry, from transportation and accommodation management to human resources and destination development.

This educational portal offers a structured, hierarchical approach to learning all course content, with:

  • Detailed explanations of every concept and topic
  • Real-world examples from leading hospitality brands (Hilton, Kyoto, etc.)
  • Visual aids including flowcharts, diagrams, and organized charts
  • Exam preparation materials including key points, quick revision boxes, and summaries
  • Mobile-responsive design for studying on any device
  • Accordion sections for easy navigation and topic expansion

🎯 Course Learning Outcomes (CO)

Upon completion of this course, students will be able to:

CO1: Fundamental Knowledge

Understand the basic concepts and functions of management in the tourism and hospitality industry

CO2: Transportation Systems

Analyze different transportation modes and their role in the tourism ecosystem

CO3: Accommodation Operations

Evaluate accommodation classifications and hotel departmental operations comprehensively

CO4: Human Resources

Apply HRM principles effectively in tourism and hospitality organizations

CO5: Destination Management

Develop sustainable destination strategies and stakeholder engagement approaches

CO6: Contemporary Trends

Understand and apply emerging technologies and trends in tourism and hospitality
